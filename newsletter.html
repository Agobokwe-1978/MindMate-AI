Mental Health Tip of the Month

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

When feeling anxious, practice this simple technique: Name 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, and 1 thing you can taste. This helps bring your focus back to the present moment.
